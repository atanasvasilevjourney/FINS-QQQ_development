## Notes

1. **Time Zones**: All times are in broker server time. Adjust according to your broker's server time.

2. **Risk Management**: 
   - Start with conservative settings
   - Never risk more than 2% of account balance per trade
   - Adjust risk amount based on account size

3. **Range Size**: 
   - Too small: May result in false breakouts
   - Too large: May result in very small position sizes
   - Adjust based on instrument volatility

4. **Testing**: 
   - Always backtest before live trading
   - Test on demo account first
   - Monitor performance over several weeks

5. **Market Conditions**:
   - Works best in trending markets
   - Avoid during major news events
   - Consider market holidays and low liquidity periods

## Quick Setup Guide

1. **Choose Your Instrument**: Select from the categories above
2. **Select Settings**: Start with conservative settings
3. **Adjust Risk**: Set risk amount based on your account size
4. **Test**: Run on demo account for at least 1 week
5. **Optimize**: Adjust settings based on performance
6. **Go Live**: Start with small risk amounts

## Performance Expectations

- **Win Rate**: Typically 40-60%
- **Risk/Reward**: Usually 1:1 to 1:3
- **Best Markets**: Trending markets with clear breakouts
- **Worst Markets**: Sideways, choppy markets
- **Timeframe**: Works best on M1, M5, M15 charts

Remember: Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always trade responsibly and within your risk tolerance.
